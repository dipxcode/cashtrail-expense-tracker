Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025 · We keep it simple and honest.

1. Data We Collect

CashTrail collects only what you provide:

  • Your name and email address (for your account)
  • Financial transaction data you enter manually (expenses, income, categories)
  • Optional profile information (phone, bio, profile picture)
  • Basic technical data like your IP address for security logging

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the CashTrail service. Specifically:

  • To display your expense and income records in the app
  • To generate analytics and charts based on your transactions
  • To provide AI insights — only when you click "Generate Insights"
  • To send you transactional emails (e.g., password reset)

We never sell, share, or monetize your data.

3. Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • All passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms (PBKDF2) — never stored in plain text
  • All data is stored on secure servers with access controls
  • HTTPS encryption is enforced in production
  • AI analysis prompts only contain anonymised aggregated totals — never raw transaction titles

4. AI & Third-Party Services

If you configure an AI key (Groq or Google Gemini), CashTrail will send a summary of your spending totals to that service when you request insights. No individual transaction titles or personal identifiers are sent. These are free-tier services governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Groq: https://groq.com/privacy
  • Google Gemini: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Export all your data as CSV at any time from your profile
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Update or correct your information from your profile page

6. Cookies

CashTrail uses only essential session cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies.

7. Contact

For any privacy concerns or data requests, please reach out through the app or contact the developer directly. We respond to all privacy requests within 48 hours.

Simple summary

Your data is yours. We store it to run the app, never sell it, and you can take it or delete it anytime.
